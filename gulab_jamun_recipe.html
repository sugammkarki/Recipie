Gulab Jamun Recipe

Ingredients:

    1 cup milk powder 1/4 cup all-purpose flour A pinch of baking soda 1 tablespoon ghee 1/4 cup milk (approximately) Oil or ghee for frying

For Syrup:

    1 cup sugar 1 cup water A few cardamom pods or rose water for flavor

Cooking Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine milk powder, all-purpose flour, baking powder, and a pinch of baking soda.
  2. Gradually add the ghee and milk to form a smooth dough. The dough should not be too sticky or too dry. If needed, add more milk or ghee as required.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for about 15-20 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, prepare the sugar syrup by dissolving sugar in water and bringing it to a boil. Add a few cardamom pods or rose water to enhance the flavor.
  5. Divide the dough into small equal-sized balls and roll them between your palms to make smooth balls. Make sure there are no cracks in the balls.
  6. Heat the oil or ghee in a deep pan over medium heat. Fry the gulab jamun balls in batches until they turn golden brown and are fully cooked from inside.
  7. Remove the fried gulab jamuns and immediately drop them into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 1-2 hours to absorb the sweetness.
  8. Serve the soft and spongy Gulab Jamuns warm or at room temperature. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds if desired. Enjoy this delectable Indian dessert!
